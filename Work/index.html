Welcome to Paper (Book & Paper) Restoration Design!

Our company working more than 20 years on the bookbinding and restoration field

As a 20 year experienced conservator, Slava is confident to work with antique and new books in cloth and leather bindings as well as maps and documents printed on paper.

Slava began working with paper in 1997 when he was a student at the Holy Trinity Seminary Russian Orthodox monastery at Jordanville New York accredited by State University of New York. He got a job at the monastery bindery, where monks of the monastery taught me how to restore antique religious books and how to bind the new ones. Since then books and paper became his passion.

He mastered his abilities with the help of very experienced and well-known masters in this field James Twomey professional bookbinder and conservator (professional associate of American Institute for Conservation), Scott Kellar (independent professional bookbinder) who taught Slava blind and gold hand tooling of fine leather bindings.

Services

The deacidification and restoration of: Rare and antique books Family Bibles including a vast collection of replacement covers, clasps, genealogy pages, and spines Church records Special collections of Research Libraries, State and Local Archives, Historical Libraries, and Seminaries Complete treatment and encapsulation of documents. Conservation storage boxes for books. Digital scanning and archival formatting of books and documents. On-site evaluation and assessment of collections. Workshops on basic book conservation skills. Seminars and presentations to increase conservation awareness. Limited edition bookbinding. Conservation of wall maps and prints.
