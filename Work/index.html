Welcome to Book Restoration Design!

Our company working more than 20 years on the bookbinding and restoration field

As a 20 year experienced conservator, Slava is confident to work with antique and new books in cloth and leather bindings as well as maps and documents printed on paper.

Slava began working with paper in 1997 when he was a student at the Holy Trinity Seminary Russian Orthodox monastery at Jordanville New York accredited by State University of New York. He got a job at the monastery bindery, where monks of the monastery taught me how to restore antique religious books and how to bind the new ones. Since then books and paper became his passion.

He mastered his abilities with the help of very experienced and well-known masters in this field James Twomey professional bookbinder and conservator (professional associate of American Institute for Conservation), Scott Kellar (independent professional bookbinder) who taught Slava blind and gold hand tooling of fine leather bindings.

Leather binding:

full bindings-bindings in which the entire cover (back and sides) is formed of the same kind of material (leather, cloth, paper)

half bindings-bindings in which the spane, a portion of the sides, and the corner of the cover are formed of one kind of material (usually leather) and the rest of the sides of another (such as cloth or paper).

Quarter bindings-binding in which the backbone and small portion of the sides are covered with one kind of materials, and the rest of the sides, including the corners, with another.

Three quarter bindings-binding in which the backbone,the corners, and a variable but fairly large portion of the sides are covered with one kind of materials, and the rest of the sides with another.

Custom made binding:

Gold stamping / Gold tooling-the process of decorating leather or other materials with hand finishing tools, which are applied to the surface of the material with hand pressure. This could be done with using real gold leaf or gold foil.

Blind Tooling-the same process like gold stamping without the use gold leaf, foil or other colouring materials.

Headband-sewn by hand over strip of leather

marbled paper-paper decorated by transferring to its surface intermingled colours which have been floated on gum solution, or size
