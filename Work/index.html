Our Philosophy

Let’s take a look at the history, at the past of the mankind. How we know what we know? How we know what happened in the past? How are we learning about our private, personal past?

The answer is memory and storages of this memory. When we involve the one to compare what happened in the past, to annalyse it and according to the result of analysis to make conclusion and based on this knowledge to guide new steps, new choices. We believe that storage of information, that store in human artifacts, the drawings on the stones, than writings on different kind surfaces such as clay, stone, wood, papyrus, bones, silk and many more… and one of them is paper… and after paper in the form of the book as we known it as perfect storage of the information, a time capsule, at first.

The books are the mirror of the modern generation. Moral and spiritual condition of the population could be seeing through the published books. By studying what people before us wrought and read it helps to understand human history. Once somebody said: “Show me your friends I will tell you who you are” and we can rephrase that a little “Show me your books and I will tell you who you are”.

The goal of our company is to help our customers to protect their most pressures sources of memory. which could be photo albums, dairies, favorite books, documents, letters.

About Us

By preserving the past, we are saving the future.

Slava Vovkovskiy

In 1996 Slava (Vyacheslav) Vovkovskiy has immigrated to the US with his family. In 1997 he has started his education at Holy Trinity Seminary which was a part of State University of New York at Albany. Slava was studying theology and in order to pay his tuition he has decided to start working at the Holy Trinity Monastery Jordanville, NY, where seminary is located.

The place where he was put to work was a book restoration bindery. Where Slava started working as apprentice for monks and older students who was restoring new printed and old (antique) books for Monastery use such as old Gospels, Prayers Books and Theological Academic literature.

This is how life of a professional book and paper conservator has begun. What Slava says: since High school I firmly decided that I want to become a psychologist. I was very much interested about it.

When Slava commenced his way of life known as restoration and conservation Kateryna was still living in Ukraine. Slava and Kateryna got married. She came to US with her daughter. As usual when you start your life from scratch, especially in a new country you have to find a job. Slava managed to help his wife to be part of this great team, which will provide many great new opportunities for all those people which are aware of such treasure. After a short time she has realized something: restoration and conservation was exactly what she was looking for to do in her life. Now whole family (Slava, Kateryna and Mary) forms one big part of Book Restoration Design LLC.
